Frosthand – Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2025

Frosthand ("the app") is a single-player card game developed by Vishal Ramachandran. This policy explains what data the app does and does not collect.

Data We Collect

Frosthand does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The app has no accounts, no sign-in, and no analytics.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app saves the following data locally on your device only using Android's standard storage:

  • Your game scores and round history
  • Personal records (lowest hand, win streak)
  • App settings (e.g. haptic feedback preference)

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Permissions

  • INTERNET – Required by the React Native framework. The app does not make any network requests.
  • VIBRATE – Used for haptic feedback when tapping cards.

Third-Party Services

Frosthand does not use any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or analytics services.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.
